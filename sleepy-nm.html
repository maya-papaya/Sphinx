Sleepy.
Noelle Mascera

She lies there in the water,
Her immaculate face engulfed by dirt.
Her circular base shown,
her body on the side.
Her precious self can't seem to find
who she is,
obviously everyone knows she is a cone.
She protects and attacks,
she fears and heals.
But what else can she do?
Can she be more
Than what the people believe?
Sadly she'll
Never know,
she never found her password.
She gave up on cracking the code.
This was Her end,
this time she didn't turn.
And now She lies in water
as the sky gets darker.
Engulfed with shame and dirt,
she wonders Who cares?
For she isn't on the corner
Anymore.
But to everyone she just lies in the Darkening river,
with no means.
